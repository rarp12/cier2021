Control and Surveillance

 

Objective

This window allows the user to identify the type of vulnerability of institutional lands keeping in mind the existence and condition of enclosure related to public order of educational institutions based on the following matrix:

 

This query also can identify the existence of complementary security systems, which is in reference to the availability of lightning and outdoor cameras.

 

Features

·         Lightning System: this variable allows you to see information on the existence of outdoor lighting systems in institutional lands, it is selected according to the case: YES or NO  

 

·         Surveillance camera system: this variable allows you to see information on the existence of outdoor surveillance camera systems in each of the buildings of institutional lands, in reference to: YES or NO

 

·         Public Order Condition: this variable defines the probability  level of the existence of problems regarding public order in the surroundings of the institutional land, its associated on a scale from 1 to 4, keeping in mind the following definitions:

 

4-it is located farther than the minimum distance (Regulation) from the identified risk.
3- It is located at the minimum distance (Regulation) from the identified risk.
2-it is located below the minimum distance (Regulation)   from the identified risk (partial affectations).            
1- It is located below the minimum distance (Regulation)   from the identified risk (total affectations).

 

·         Sistema Iluminación: Esta variable permite mostrar la información sobre la existencia de sistemas de iluminación exterior en cada uno de los edificios de los predios educativos, se selecciona acorde al caso: SI o NO

 

·         Sistema Cámaras: Esta variable permite mostrar la información sobre la existencia de sistemas de cámara exterior en cada uno de los edificios de los predios educativos, en referencia a: SI o NO

 

·         Condición Orden Público :Esta variable determina el nivel de probabilidad de existencia de problemas de orden público en el entorno que se encuentra el predio educativo, está asociada a una escala del 1 al 4, teniendo en cuenta las siguientes definiciones:

4-Se encuentra ubicado a más de la distancia mínima (Normativa) del riesgo identificado.
3- Se encuentra ubicado a la distancia mínima  (Normativa) del  riesgo identificado.
2- Se encuentra ubicado por debajo de la distancia mínima (Normativa) del riesgo identificado, (afectaciones parciales).
1-  Se encuentra ubicado por debajo de la distancia mínima (Normativa) del riesgo o identificado, (afectaciones totales).

·      Vulnerability: this variable defines how vulnerable the institutional land is regarding the condition of enclosure and public order existing in its surroundings, the following matrix is used as a reference.

 

 

With the following reference values: high, medium and Low.
