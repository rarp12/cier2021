Objective

This window shows the user the possible owners that may run or own the institutional land, it also shows those that may have any legal/ownership problems. Finally this query allows the user to analyze the impact of institutional lands with ownership/legalization problems by defining the affected population by this situation.

 

Atributos

·         ownership validation: this field allows us to see information regarding the possibility to validate data on the ownership of the land, in reference to the following terms:

1.       YES ,

2.       NO  or

3.       UNVERIFIABLE

Institutional lands presenting ownership/legalization problems will be those whose ownership wasnt possible to validate (UNVERIFIABLE) and those with no owner (NO)

·         Type of owner: this query refers to the territorial or legal entity/person who legally owns the property, in reference to: 


1.       Family welfare fund,

2.       Community,

3.       Cooperatives,

4.       Corporation,

5.       unknown,

6.       Educational Establishment,

7.       Federation,

8.       Foundation,

9.       Others,

10.   Private,

11.   Public,  

12.   Municipality.
