 EXTERNAL ACCESSIBILITY

 

Objective

This window provides information regarding the types of access to the lands and their condition. To define its level of compliance, keeping in mind the following definition:

Does Comply, the institutional land that has at least one access in good condition, meaning three (3) or four (4), in the other cases it doesn’t comply. 

This consultation also allows us to quantify the number of accesses in bad condition, meaning one (1) or two (2) as well as the number of different types of transportation media used to get to the institution. Finally this window lets us see the population affected by lands which do not have good accessibility, in order to analyze the impact of the situation.

Features 

·         Accessibility Condition: this variable assesses the condition of the accesses to the land, keeping in mind that there can be up to four different types, in reference to:

Does Comply (has at least one access in conditions 3 or 4)

Does not comply (does not even have one access in conditions 3 or 4)

 

·        Types of Transportation Media: this area permits the identification of transportation media which enables the access to institutional lands, keeping in mind the following classification: Animal (e.g. A horse), Car, Bicycle, School Bus, Boat/raft/canoe, Motorcycle, Pedestrian Walkway, Public Transportation.
