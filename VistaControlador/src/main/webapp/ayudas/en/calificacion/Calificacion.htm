1.      Rating

 

Objective

This query lets the user know a rating ranking for each land or group of them according to the levels of aggregation. The value derives from applying a model which allows every country to establish criteria of relevance regarding aspects of educational infrastructure, and provides a score for each scope by assigning percentages according to the importance stipulated among variables as well as a total score which depends on the weighting that the country parametrizes for each scope.    

It is important to consider that when assigning percentages to the variables, the addition of these percentages cannot exceed 100% and it isnt mandatory to assign a percentage to all of the scopes.   

For the risk scope, the calculation of the rating is a penalty, which means, according to the risks affecting the land or a group of them, it is subtracted from the other obtained results.  

 

Entry

To make a query by Generalities, you must click on query and select Rating on the drop-down list,
