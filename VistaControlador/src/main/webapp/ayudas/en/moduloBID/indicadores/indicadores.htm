HELP WINDOW INDICATORS CONSULTING

This module gives the user the consulting grade option of each one of the countries in different aspects from the managing BID’s module.

In order to access the consulting indicators option, we have to Access the BID’s module in the application’s main menu.

 


Once in the side-menu module click on the indicator’s option.

 

 

 

 

 


In the option the next window will pop-up.


Located on the screen’s upper-part, select as first step the period which will have data consulted, in addition to this in the right part different areas are shown which load indicators, once selected click on the consulting button.

Then the list will show up the assigned information for each one of the countries, areas and indicators.


Additionally to this you have the option to hide or show the columns, the first time to be consulted the available columns will show up, to hide or show you have to select the group located in the upper part of the results’ table and click on the mark.


Functionality is the exporting to Excel (XLS) of the generated inform, for this click on the export button located in the upper-right part of the results’ table.
